ARTBAT

ARTBAT duo consists of two experienced Kiev-based DJs and producers Artur and Batish. They joined in 2016 and already have a good reputation in the world of electronic music. For example, the duo performed at the top of the Brazilian mountain as part of the Cercle Evening Series. The video of their set had more than two million views on YouTube.

DJ Awards

Ukrainians were nominated for the DJ Awards in the "Breakthrough of the Year" category. This is one of the most prestigious awards in the world of electronic music. The award ceremony took place on Ibiza Island.
